I am a Research Associate in the Department of Electronic & Electrical Engineering at the University of Strathclyde, with a PhD in Computing Science from the University of Stirling and a Master of Engineering in Software Engineering from Heriot-Watt University. My research focuses on image processing, computer vision, and edge AI, particularly for industrial and scientific applications. I have experience developing domain-specific optimisations of image processing algorithms on novel heterogeneous architectures.

My expertise spans areas like computer vision (object detection, classification), spiking neural networks, hyperspectral imaging, and hardware performance optimisation, with a keen interest in exploring the balance between computational efficiency, energy consumption, and algorithmic performance.
